# PWA Skills Trial — Phase 11: Mission Content & Authoring System

This document specifies the structural content rules, templates, and specialization branches configured for the launch content pack.

---

## 1. Universal Launch Project Story Arc
The primary project story arc is centered around a fictional community client, **Grace Community Center**, planning a neighborhood event.

* **Client Name:** Grace Community Center  
* **Storyline:** The client is launching a "Community Impact Day" next month. They need a public announcement newsletter drafted, a registration calendar link set up, an internal checklist established, and a handoff update prepared. The kickoff notes are deliberately messy to test active observation.

---

## 2. Mandatory Mission Templates (Seeded)

### Step 1: Welcome to Pure Water
* **Kind:** `learn`
* **Est. Time:** 30 minutes
* **Summary:** Introduction to PWA's mission, values (Teach through work, Systems Scout), and structure. Gated by a scenario check matching process triage.

### Step 2: Sandbox Console Practice
* **Kind:** `tour`
* **Est. Time:** 45 minutes
* **Summary:** Active practice task where candidates learn to transition task statuses, write comments, attach evidence URLs, and submit work in the native VA Manager interface.

### Step 3: How We Keep Promises
* **Kind:** `learn`
* **Est. Time:** 45 minutes
* **Summary:** Orientation on reliability protocols, communication channels, ETAs, and blocker-reporting practices.

### Step 4: AI and Safety Guidelines
* **Kind:** `learn`
* **Est. Time:** 45 minutes
* **Summary:** Guidance on acceptable AI use (verify figures/links, disclose drafts, do not paste client credentials or private donor details).

### Step 5: Client Sim — Community Impact Day
* **Kind:** `sim`
* **Est. Time:** 105 minutes
* **Summary:** Kickoff email review, date contradiction identification, clarification message draft, newsletter announcement editing, and a formal task status handoff.

### Step 6: Specialization Branch
* **Kind:** `branch`
* **Est. Time:** 120 minutes
* **Summary:** Specialized task assigned based on application track.

### Step 7: Thursday Newsletter SOP
* **Kind:** `sop`
* **Est. Time:** 75 minutes
* **Summary:** Writing an operational SOP (Purpose, Inputs, Steps, Exceptions, DoD) from a process walkthrough, and identifying one simplification suggestion.

### Step 8: Team Standup Meeting
* **Kind:** `meet`
* **Est. Time:** 30 minutes
* **Summary:** simulated 15-minute meeting where candidate confirms schedule, arrives prepared, and posts a formal status handoff.

### Step 9: Walkthrough & Reflection
* **Kind:** `reflect`
* **Est. Time:** 60 minutes
* **Summary:** Reflection questionnaire (key design choices, adjustments with more time, learning self-assessment).

## 3. Specialization Tracks Details

### Track A: Communications / Newsletter
* **Task:** Draft the June Ministry newsletter.
* **Brief:** Candidate is given 5 rough calendar submissions. They must edit the layout, cross-verify dates, flag a missing speaker time, and draft the email draft for client approval.

### Track B: Project Coordination
* **Task:** Evolving Notes into a Project Schedule.
* **Brief:** Convert raw, messy planning notes into a structural task hierarchy in the console, assigning mock owners, calculating milestones, and flagging resource bottlenecks.

### Track C: Grant & Nonprofit Research
* **Task:** Mock Foundation directory search.
* **Brief:** Research three specific mock grant programs for a youth community clinic. Cross-check eligibility rules, document funding amounts, and write a summary brief.
