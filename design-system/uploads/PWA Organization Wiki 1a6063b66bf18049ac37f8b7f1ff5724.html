🛠️ Internal: Wiki Maintenance

This section is for editors of this wiki — not part of the new-hire orientation.

  • Template for client organizations

    This PWA Organization Wiki should become the model for client and partner workspaces. When adapted for another organization, keep the same pattern:

    1. Organization Snapshot
    1. Mission, Vision, and Values
    1. Key Policies Index
    1. People, Roles, and Teams
    1. Official Services or Programs
    1. Client / Member / Community Delivery Philosophy
    1. Current Priorities

    Rule for reuse: copy the structure and operating principles, but link each organization's own canonical databases and source pages instead of duplicating PWA's internal records.

  • Remaining build checklist
    • Confirm exact legal entity name
    • Link every policy row to a canonical SOP or policy page
    • Link leadership rows to the canonical leadership/team database when ready
    • Create or link a Communication Expectations policy
    • Create or link an AI Usage and Review Standards policy
    • Create a cleaned reusable client/org version of this wiki template
